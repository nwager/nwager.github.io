About Me

I am a third-year student in Computer Engineering at the University of Washington, Seattle (UW). I have experience in both software and hardware roles, with skills in front-end development and electronics.

Design, Build, Fly

I'm an active member of Design, Build, Fly UW, an engineering club at UW. Dozens of students collaborate in subteams to design and manufacture a mid-size RC airplane to compete with other schools in a competition hosted by the AIAA.
As a member of the Avionics team, I am responsible for creating wiring schematics, assembling and soldering electrical components, and installing the flight controller and control surfaces.

Music

When I'm not working on projects, I spend much of my free time playing electric bass and guitar. I started on bass in 2012 and began learning guitar in high school, playing covers at School of Rock, Portland. I currently play bass for The Sleepy Haunts, an indie-pop band with rock influence.
